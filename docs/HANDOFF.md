## Architecture reference

- **Monetary system (GDD В§5.1):** `tickMonetary()` runs every `monthlyUpdate` once `central_bank_charter` is enacted. `privateLeverage` drifts: `dLeverage = (NEUTRAL_RATE(5%) вҲ’ policyRate) Г— 0.5 Г— (1 вҲ’ leverage/5.0)` per tick вҖ” below neutral rate leverage builds, above it contracts. `confidence` mean-reverts to 70, with pressure from `privateLeverage Г— policyRate > 0.18` (the fragility threshold) and from `inflationRate > 0.08`. Below confidence 30 + leverage > 0.5 вҶ’ deleveraging (leverage Г— 0.05вҖ“0.11 per month). `inflationRate` drifts toward `0.02 + boom_component + printInflation(1%/mo if print)`; `monetaryRegime` = `float`|`peg`|`print`. `exchangeRate` (0.3вҖ“2.0): float drifts with trade balance, rate differential, confidence; peg holds fixed but drains treasury on deficit (breaks when treasury < 0.25Г—GDP); print depreciates вҲ’1.2%/mo. GDP in `monthlyEconomy` now multiplied by credit-cycle factor (confidence > 50 вҶ’ +0.2% per point above 50; confidence < 30 вҶ’ вҲ’0.4% per point below 30) and inflation drag (above 8% annual: GDP Г— (1 вҲ’ excess Г— 2)). Bank interest = `treasury Г— policyRate/12` (was hardcoded 0.005). Export earnings Г— `1/exchangeRate` FX boost. `bondRate = policyRate + CREDIT_RATING_SPREADS[creditRating]`; `issueBonds(amount)` gates on creditRating вү  D and debt < 200% annual GDP. `crashFired` flag prevents 1929-analog from firing twice. **Note:** `tickMonetary` consumes RNG draws during the deleveraging-bust and peg-break paths вҖ” event streams shift in saves where credit is fragile.
- **Climate (GDD В§8.2):** `tickClimate()` runs in every `monthlyUpdate`. `co2ppm` += `playerEmissions()` (pop-scaled tech intensity; renewables Г—0.6, fusion Г—0.15, Carbon Levy Г—0.7) + `worldEmissions()` (rival pop вүҘ 12k floor Г— 1905вҖ“1960 ramp Г— post-2030 decarb Г— diffusion Г— accordFactor вҖ” the player's green research and accords both bend the *world* curve). `warmingC` closes 1/40 of the gap to `(ppmвҲ’295)Г—0.011` per tick (20-year lag, 2 ticks/yr), then geoengineering cooling applies if active. `tickAccords()` runs immediately after warming update: each accord signatory's `accordCompliance` drifts (commerceГ—0.006 вҲ’ expansionГ—0.006 Вұ noise); below `ACCORD_DEFECT_THRESHOLD` (0.35) вҶ’ one-time log entry + sanction verb. `deployGeoengineering()` sets `geoDeployed`/`geoDeployDay` and hits every rival вҲ’15 relations; the cooling applies for `GEOENGINEER_DURATION_DAYS` (120) days = 4 climate ticks, shedding `GEOENGINEER_COOLING / 4` = 0.1В°C per tick. All new fields serialized with `??` defaults. **Note:** `tickClimate()` now consumes 1 RNG draw per accord signatory вҖ” this shifts event streams in saves that have accords. Impacts: `climateDrag` on crops past +0.8В°C, washout chance Г—(1+0.3Г—warming) capped 0.3, tidal flooding (year вүҘ 2035, warming > 1.5, coastal, no `seaWall`). At 2040 `decideBranch()`: proj вүҘ 2.3 вҶ’ drowned; else non-democratic / avgSat < 42 / legitimacy < 35 вҶ’ dystopia; else solarpunk. At 2100 `buildCenturyReport()`.
- **Route kinds:** `'trail' | 'road' | 'rail' | 'highway' | 'maglev'`; `KIND_RANK` enforces upgrade-only; `buildLink` refuses downgrades.
- **Gates:** `railUnlocked()` = `stateProclaimed && year >= 1912`; highway = State + year вүҘ 1945; maglev = State + year вүҘ 2005 (each вҲ’5 years with its tech node). `maintBill(r)` is Г—0.6 once `automated_logistics` is researched.
- **Save format:** v2 `{v:2, mode:'region', town, region}` under `centuria-save`; v1 town saves still load.
- **Clocks:** town = 4 game-min/tick; region = 30 game-min/tick (~6 s/day at speed 1).
- **Effective route capacity:** `capacity Г— condition/100`; condition floor 15; unpaid maintenance вҲ’6/mo; washouts вҲ’45 condition on storm days (12% chance), `repairRoute` restores to 100 from treasury.
- **Relief line:** `reliefLine(t)` = larger town reachable via road/rail вҶ’ Г—1.25 militia in raid branch of `fireEvent`.
- **Markets:** `routePath` with `usable` filter; arbitrage fires when margin > 1.5Г— freight (ВЈ0.01/unit/hop); clamped to remaining capacity after caravans; 5% State levy into treasury.
- **Policy effects:** ongoing monthly in `monthlyEconomy`; faction wiring in `updateFactions`.
- **Region events:** `routePath` for highwaymen вҖ” freight gate means quiet routes carry nothing worth robbing; earlier draft robbed subsistence and starved the harness.
- **Diplomacy:** rivals emerge in `updateDiplomacy` (monthly) from 1922, вүӨ6, archetype presets over В§6.3 weights; regimes from `RIVAL_REGIMES` (10, era-gated, 4 blocs; `blocAffinity` feeds both player and pair baselines); treaty acceptance = `relations вүҘ treatyAsk()` (personality-priced, +15/breach reputation penalty); hostile = relations < вҲ’40 with no NAP вҶ’ sponsored raids (Г—1.3 strength, 50% per raid) and border friction; trade agreements pay `exportEarningsLastMonth` in `monthlyEconomy` (Г—1.5 during `warBoomUntil`).
- **The world's own politics:** `rivalPairs` (keyed `minId:maxId`) drift in `tickForeignRelations`; pairs > 45 + honor ally, > 25 + commerce open customs unions, < вҲ’20 trade ultimatums, < вҲ’50 вҶ’ `startForeignWar` (240вҖ“720 days; refugee waves 20%/mo; peace bleeds the loser ~10вҖ“15% pop, sets the pair at вҲ’60, 50% defeat-topples its regime via era-gated `pickRegime`). Alliances block war within the pair and harden sides when wars start. Rival `history[]` accrues beats (capped 16, founding line kept).
- **Negotiation engine (GDD В§6.3):** `evaluateDeal(rv, basket)` is pure (UI live-previews); basket = `{treaties, goldToThem, goldToYou, borderSettlement}`. Value to the rival: `treatyAppetite` (trade = commerceГ—1.6вҲ’4; NAP = (10вҲ’risk)Г—0.8+honorГ—0.3вҲ’3; pact = honorГ—0.8вҲ’riskГ—0.5вҲ’5), `borderAppetite` = (4вҲ’expansion)Г—1.2+grudgeГ—0.4, gold at `(0.6+commerceГ—0.08)/GOLD_PER_POINT(8)` per ВЈ. Accept when `get вүҘ giveГ—premium + tableCost`; premium = 1+breachesГ—0.3+grudgeГ—0.03вҲ’relations/150 (floor 0.5); tableCost = max(0,вҲ’relations/8)+breachesГ—2+grudgeГ—0.4. Within 30% вҶ’ counter-offer (gold sweetener, stored in `counters`, 90-day expiry, signable via `acceptCounter`); else walk with reason. `borderSettled` on the rival kills border friction + border CB (both ways: AI declares with `fabricated` instead) and adds +6 relations drift. `proposeTreaty` (single-item, relations-threshold) still exists alongside.
- **War depth (GDD В§7.3вҖ“7.4):** `tickPlayerWar` skips the declaration day (`startedDay === day`) вҖ” the AI declares inside the same monthly tick, and resolving immediately made support assertions flaky (this was main's red CI). Blockade: `setBlockade` needs militia вүҘ 2 or standing army; enemy power Г—0.85 + pop Г—0.997 + score +1.5/mo; costs `popГ—0.02`/mo and exports Г—0.6 (Г—0.7 in any war вҖ” contested lanes). Co-belligerence: `callAlly` on defensive-pact rivals вҖ” defensive war: honors if honor вүҘ 4 (else honor/10 chance, refusal tears the pact publicly); offensive: needs relations вүҘ 60 + honor-weighted chance. Called allies fight at `pop^0.6Г—0.5` (passive pacts 0.25), bleed at half lossRate, share peace (+8) and defeat (вҲ’10). Enemy alliance partners join at war start with `0.3+honorГ—0.05` chance, add `pop^0.6Г—0.3`.
