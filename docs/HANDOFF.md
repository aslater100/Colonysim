## Architecture reference

- **Monetary system (GDD ¬ß5.1):** `tickMonetary()` runs every `monthlyUpdate` once `central_bank_charter` is enacted. `privateLeverage` drifts: `dLeverage = (NEUTRAL_RATE(5%) ‚àí policyRate) √ó 0.5 √ó (1 ‚àí leverage/5.0)` per tick ‚Äî below neutral rate leverage builds, above it contracts. `confidence` mean-reverts to 70, with pressure from `privateLeverage √ó policyRate > 0.18` (the fragility threshold) and from `inflationRate > 0.08`. Below confidence 30 + leverage > 0.5 ‚Üí deleveraging (leverage √ó 0.05‚Äì0.11 per month). `inflationRate` drifts toward `0.02 + boom_component + printInflation(1%/mo if print)`; `monetaryRegime` = `float`|`peg`|`print`. `exchangeRate` (0.3‚Äì2.0): float drifts with trade balance, rate differential, confidence; peg holds fixed but drains treasury on deficit (breaks when treasury < 0.25√óGDP); print depreciates ‚àí1.2%/mo. GDP in `monthlyEconomy` now multiplied by credit-cycle factor (confidence > 50 ‚Üí +0.2% per point above 50; confidence < 30 ‚Üí ‚àí0.4% per point below 30) and inflation drag (above 8% annual: GDP √ó (1 ‚àí excess √ó 2)). Bank interest = `treasury √ó policyRate/12` (was hardcoded 0.005). Export earnings √ó `1/exchangeRate` FX boost. `bondRate = policyRate + CREDIT_RATING_SPREADS[creditRating]`; `issueBonds(amount)` gates on creditRating ‚âÝ D and debt < 200% annual GDP. `crashFired` flag prevents 1929-analog from firing twice. **Note:** `tickMonetary` consumes RNG draws during the deleveraging-bust and peg-break paths ‚Äî event streams shift in saves where credit is fragile.
- **Climate (GDD ¬ß8.2):** `tickClimate()` runs in every `monthlyUpdate`. `co2ppm` += `playerEmissions()` (pop-scaled tech intensity; renewables √ó0.6, fusion √ó0.15, Carbon Levy √ó0.7) + `worldEmissions()` (rival pop ‚â• 12k floor √ó 1905‚Äì1960 ramp √ó post-2030 decarb √ó diffusion √ó accordFactor ‚Äî the player's green research and accords both bend the *world* curve). `warmingC` closes 1/40 of the gap to `(ppm‚àí295)√ó0.011` per tick (20-year lag, 2 ticks/yr), then geoengineering cooling applies if active. `tickAccords()` runs immediately after warming update: each accord signatory's `accordCompliance` drifts (commerce√ó0.006 ‚àí expansion√ó0.006 ¬± noise); below `ACCORD_DEFECT_THRESHOLD` (0.35) ‚Üí one-time log entry + sanction verb. `deployGeoengineering()` sets `geoDeployed`/`geoDeployDay` and hits every rival ‚àí15 relations; the cooling applies for `GEOENGINEER_DURATION_DAYS` (120) days = 4 climate ticks, shedding `GEOENGINEER_COOLING / 4` = 0.1¬∞C per tick. All new fields serialized with `??` defaults. **Note:** `tickClimate()` now consumes 1 RNG draw per accord signatory ‚Äî this shifts event streams in saves that have accords. Impacts: `climateDrag` on crops past +0.8¬∞C, washout chance √ó(1+0.3√ówarming) capped 0.3, tidal flooding (year ‚â• 2035, warming > 1.5, coastal, no `seaWall`). At 2040 `decideBranch()`: proj ‚â• 2.3 ‚Üí drowned; else non-democratic / avgSat < 42 / legitimacy < 35 ‚Üí dystopia; else solarpunk. At 2100 `buildCenturyReport()`.
- **Route kinds:** `'trail' | 'road' | 'rail' | 'highway' | 'maglev'`; `KIND_RANK` enforces upgrade-only; `buildLink` refuses downgrades.
- **Gates:** `railUnlocked()` = `stateProclaimed && year >= 1912`; highway = State + year ‚â• 1945; maglev = State + year ‚â• 2005 (each ‚àí5 years with its tech node). `maintBill(r)` is √ó0.6 once `automated_logistics` is researched.

- **Effective route capacity:** `capacity √ó condition/100`; condition floor 15; unpaid maintenance ‚àí6/mo; washouts ‚àí45 condition on storm days (12% chance), `repairRoute` restores to 100 from treasury.
- **Relief line:** `reliefLine(t)` = larger town reachable via road/rail ‚Üí √ó1.25 militia in raid branch of `fireEvent`.

- **Diplomacy:** rivals emerge in `updateDiplomacy` (monthly) from 1922, ‚â§6, archetype presets over ¬ß6.3 weights; regimes from `RIVAL_REGIMES` (10, era-gated, 4 blocs; `blocAffinity` feeds both player and pair baselines); treaty acceptance = `relations ‚â• treatyAsk()` (personality-priced, +15/breach reputation penalty); hostile = relations < ‚àí40 with no NAP ‚Üí sponsored raids (√ó1.3 strength, 50% per raid) and border friction; trade agreements pay `exportEarningsLastMonth` in `monthlyEconomy` (√ó1.5 during `warBoomUntil`).
